Our Mission, Vision, and Career opportunities

Engineering Tomorrow's Innovations, Today.

Mission

To provide students with a comprehensive education in computer engineering, emphasizing both theoretical foundations and practical skills. To foster a culture of innovation, creativity, and critical thinking in computer engineering. To develop students who can work effectively in multidisciplinary teams and communicate complex technical information to both technical and non-technical audiences. To prepare students for successful careers in industry, academia, and research, and to contribute to the advancement of the field of computer engineering.


Vision

To be a leading computer engineering program, recognized for its excellence in education, research, and community engagement. To produce graduates who are globally competent, innovative, and socially responsible. To create a collaborative and inclusive environment that fosters diversity, equity, and inclusion. To advance the field of computer engineering through research, innovation, and entrepreneurship.


Career Opportunities

Computer engineering is a discipline that encompasses knowledge from computer science and electrical engineering. It refers to the creation, development, and analysis of computer-related systems and technologies. Computer engineers have a wide range of job opportunities and can choose specializations that focus on software, hardware, or even embedded systems and networks.

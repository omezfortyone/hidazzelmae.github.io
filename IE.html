PIIE - National College of Science and Technology Student Chapter

National College of Science and Technology Student Chapter is a dynamic organization dedicated to promoting the principles and practices of industrial engineering among students. Our chapter serves as a platform for aspiring industrial engineers to enhance their skills, network with industry professionals, and make a positive impact in their communities.

Our Mission, Vision, and Career opportunities

Engineering Tomorrow's Innovations, Today.

Mission

To produce globally competitive, highly trained Industrial Engineers, who are equipped with a strong foundation on mathematics, science, methods and systems analysis and design, production systems and quality control, human resources management, and systems for the benefit of the whole organization and serve as a bridge between management goals operational performances


Vision

To be recognized as a leading educational institute with competent graduates of B.S. in Industrial Engineering who can be leaders in their respective workplace.


Career Opportunities

Industrial engineering offers diverse career opportunities in industries such as manufacturing, healthcare, logistics, and consulting. With their versatile skill set, industrial engineers play a vital role in optimizing systems and driving innovation to meet the evolving needs of businesses and society.
